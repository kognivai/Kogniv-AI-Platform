Platform Decision Framework
Kogniv evaluates each workflow against three migration paths before recommending a platform. The decision is driven by workflow domain, data gravity, AI capability requirements, and your existing licence footprint.
Path 1 — Reimagine in ServiceNow
Use NOW Assist, Predictive Intelligence, Virtual Agent, AI Search. Best when workflow is ITSM/ITOM/CSM-native, data gravity is in CMDB, and compliance requires single-platform governance.
Path 2 — Migrate to specialist platform
Move to Power Automate, Salesforce, Temporal, or n8n. Best when workflow is HR/Finance/CRM-adjacent, your org already has the target platform licensed, or ServiceNow AI capability is insufficient.
Path 3 — Hybrid orchestration
ServiceNow triggers workflow, specialist AI platform handles processing, result writes back. Best for complex multi-system flows where no single platform handles all steps.
